K.N.U.S.T. COURSE EVALUATION

This survey is to assit Academic heads, Heads of Department and the various college provost(s) on students performance.

Fill in the following


1.Overall, how would you rate the course?






2.How useful were the course materials (readings, textbooks, etc)?






3.How clearly did your instructor explain the course material?






4.Was the speed with which your instructor presented the course material too fast, too slow, or about right?






5.How well did your instructor answer students' questions






6.How will you rate your lecturers?
